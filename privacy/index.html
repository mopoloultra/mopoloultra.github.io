Ultra Quiz Privacy Policy

Transparency, privacy, and responsible data use for Ultra Quiz users.

Last Updated: May 2026
Summary: Ultra Quiz is a trivia and learning app. We do not sell your personal information. The app may store gameplay progress locally on your device and may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to show ads, measure performance, and support rewarded hints.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ultra Quiz collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the Ultra Quiz mobile application. By using Ultra Quiz, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Ultra Quiz is designed to collect only the information needed to operate, improve, and monetize the app responsibly.

  • Gameplay and app usage data: quiz interactions, selected answers, score, level progress, hints used, high scores, win streaks, app settings, selected language, and sound/theme preferences.
  • Device and technical data: device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, performance information, screen size, language preference, and approximate region where available.
  • Advertising data: advertising identifiers, ad impressions, ad clicks, rewarded ad completion, consent status, and ad performance data collected by advertising partners such as Google AdMob.
  • Contact information: if you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the content of your message so we can respond to your request.

3. Sensitive User and Device Data

Ultra Quiz does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, financial account details, precise GPS location, health data, biometric data, contacts, private messages, photos, or microphone recordings.

If any third-party service processes device identifiers or approximate location for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, or ad measurement, that processing is governed by the privacy policies and consent mechanisms of those providers.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide the quiz experience, including questions, levels, categories, difficulty filters, hints, timers, results, scores, and streaks.
  • To save preferences such as language, theme, sound, and onboarding status.
  • To improve app stability, performance, user experience, and future features.
  • To display ads, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.
  • To detect abuse, prevent invalid ad activity, and comply with legal and platform requirements.

5. Local Storage on Your Device

Some information is stored locally on your device using app storage. This may include your selected language, theme preference, sound setting, high score, level progress, streaks, and onboarding completion status.

You can remove locally stored data by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

6. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Ultra Quiz uses Google AdMob and may use related Google services to display ads and measure ad performance. Ads may include banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads that users voluntarily watch to receive in-app benefits such as hints.

  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and ad interaction data.
  • Rewarded ads are optional and user-initiated.
  • Ad providers may use data to personalize or measure ads, depending on your consent and device settings.

Google’s advertising and privacy practices are governed by Google’s own policies.

7. Consent and Your Choices

Where required by law, such as in the European Economic Area or other regulated regions, Ultra Quiz may present a consent form using Google’s User Messaging Platform or similar consent tools.

  • You may choose whether to allow personalized ads where consent options are available.
  • You can manage ad personalization from your device settings.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier from your mobile device settings.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party providers only when necessary to operate the app, show ads, analyze performance, comply with law, or protect against fraud and abuse.

9. Data Retention

Local gameplay data and settings remain on your device until you clear app data or uninstall Ultra Quiz. Data processed by third-party providers, such as advertising or analytics data, is retained according to those providers’ own retention policies.

10. Children’s Privacy

Ultra Quiz is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission, storage, or electronic processing is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where consent is used as the legal basis for processing.

For requests related to third-party advertising data, you may also need to contact the relevant provider, such as Google, directly.

13. International Data Processing

Third-party services used by Ultra Quiz may process data on servers located in different countries. By using the app, you understand that information may be processed outside your country of residence, subject to applicable laws and provider safeguards.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date on this page. Continued use of Ultra Quiz after changes means you accept the updated policy.
